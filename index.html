The Founder

Aunt Jia Jia has rescued stray dogs since 2007 and has rescued and housed more than 2,000 stray cats and dogs on less than five acres of land since 2007.

Since the number of stray dogs and cats is increasing, Aunt Jia Jia founded H.O.P.E in 2009. H.O.P.E is an offically registered animal welfare charity.

The Main Purposes

Our four main purposes are (rescue, adoption, ligation and education). The stray in the shelter are given a great deal of care and love to ensure that they are always happy and healthy.

In addition to basic needs, they will also receive adequate medical care, including ligation and routine vaccinations, just like your pets at home.

The Vision

Our vision is to make a difference in the living conditions of all animals through the emotions that stray animals inspire towards humans. Sterilize instead of killing, adopt or sponsor instead of buying pets. Change people's ideas through education and inspire people to coexist peacefully with animals.

It is hoped that the government will increase corresponding measures in rescuing stray pets and implement more stringent legislation to protect animals. To establish a harmonious relationship between humans and animals.
